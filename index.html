The Verification Framework

Professional fact-checkers use the **S.I.F.T.** and **C.P.C.D.** methods. Here is your toolkit:

01

Stop

Check your emotional bias before interacting.

02

Context

Investigate the original source of the file.

03

Provenance

Look for technical metadata or AI 'watermarks'.

04

Cross-Check

Search for the same image on trusted news wires.

05

Decide

Verify or debunk based on the evidence found.
