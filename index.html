Can You Spot AI?

Learn how to verify suspicious images in the age of AI

In a world of deepfakes and AI-generated art, seeing is no longer believing. This gallery is designed to help you build the "mental muscle" needed to distinguish between reality, AI, and intentional misinformation.

The 5-Step Verification Method

1. Stop

If a photo triggers a strong emotion, pause before sharing.

2. Context

Check the source. Who posted this and why?

3. Provenance

Find the original. Is this the oldest version of the image?

4. Cross-check

Are reputable news outlets reporting the same thing?

5. Decide

Only share if you are certain the image is authentic and true.
