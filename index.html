The Art of Verification

Before you test your skills, learn the difference between captured reality and algorithmic synthesis.

The Comparison: Real vs. AI

Authentic Photo

The Human Element

Notice the "micro-imperfections." Real skin has uneven pores, tiny stray hairs, and reflections in the eyes that match the actual environment.

AI Generated

The Algorithmic Gloss

AI often creates "perfect" skin that looks like plastic. Look at the ears or jewelry—AI frequently fails to make earrings match or makes glasses merge into the skin.

The S.I.F.T. Method

Master these 5 steps before entering the gallery.

01

Stop

Check your bias. Is the image making you angry or happy? Pause.

02

Investigate

Who is the creator? Are they a trusted journalist or a random bot?

03

Find

Find better coverage. Does the AP or Reuters have this photo?

04

Trace

Trace the original context. Use reverse image search.

05

Verify

Look for technical artifacts like warped hands or distorted text.
